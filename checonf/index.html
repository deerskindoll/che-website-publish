The first annual Eclipse Che users’ conference.

Join us on November 15th to learn how Eclipse Che, one of the fastest growing open source projects in the world, is disrupting software development. This one day virtual conference will offer insight into how cloud workspaces, powered by Docker, simplify the way individuals, teams and organizations develop software.

View All Sessions

What you will learn.

Attend this one day virtual conference to hear Bitnami, Docker, Red Hat and other open source project committers explain how Eclipse Che improves development workflow, team productivity and release frequency. You will learn how to simplify the setup and sharing of developer workspaces, and how to build custom plugins, extensions and development stacks for Che.

  • Insight
    Eclipse Che is disrupting software development. Explore what Che means to you, understand how it works, and how to use to Che for better software development.

  • Educate
    Our sessions will enlighten and educate expert and novice Che users alike. This is your chance to go deep with live tutorials and sessions on how to use Che.

  • Extend
    Hear from contributors, experts and evangelists talk about how to leverage Che’s extensible architecture, and develop custom plugins that make Che your own.

Special Thanks

Thanks to our friends at Bitnami for their contributions to the Eclipse Che open project, and sponsoring the first annual CheConf users’ conference.
