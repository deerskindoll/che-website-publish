Register for CheConf

The second annual Eclipse Che users’ conference.

This one day virtual conference brings insights into how containerized developer workspaces simplify the way individuals, teams and organizations develop software.

See the future of cloud-native development.

Attend this one day virtual conference to hear contributors and maintainers from across the Eclipse Che and cloud IDE ecosystem discuss how containerized developer workspaces can drive better development workflows, team productivity and release frequency. You'll learn how going cloud-native simplifies setup and sharing of developer workspaces as well as how to build custom plugins and language servers.

  • Insight
    Eclipse Che is bringing continuous practices to software development. Explore how Che works, and best practices to get the most from it in your team.

  • Educate
    CheConf17 is your chance to go deep into Che with live tutorials and sessions on how to use and extend Che and how to build out a language server.

  • Extend
    Organizations around the world are extending Che to drive their own developer ecosystems. Learn their approaches and hear their successes.
