= Registering to Hosted{nbsp}{prod-short}

This section describes how to register to Hosted{nbsp}{prod-short}.

.Procedure

. Navigate to link:https://che.openshift.io/[Hosted{nbsp}{prod-short}].

. Log in with an existing OpenShift{nbsp}Online, Red{nbsp}Hat Developer{nbsp}Program, or Red{nbsp}Hat Customer{nbsp}Portal account, *or* register for a new Red{nbsp}Hat account.

. Click the btn:[Activate account] button.

. Wait for an email with a link to connect to the Eclipse{nbsp}{prod-short} instance. It is sent after the environment is ready for use.

. Follow the *Get Started* link in the email.

The Hosted{nbsp}{prod-short} user dashboard is displayed.
